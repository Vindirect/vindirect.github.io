Desert Maze

Download this map for Generals Zero Hour or Generals

        

About

This is the desert-version of Gravel Maze. Textures have been replaced and light settings changed from evening to morning. Some buildings were changed and a few civilian bunkers added.

Layout

Each base area has three entrances. You can cross the map over the mountain ridges as well. There are civilian bunkers near the bases along the roads and in the mountains facing the central cavity of the map.

Supplies

You start out with one supply dock and three supply piles. Directly behind your starting position - in the very corner of the map - is an oil derrick just waiting to be captured. There are more oil derricks along the side-roads between the base locations. In the central area there are two supply docks with lots of extra cash, and an oil refinery.

AI

The AI works very well on this map. You might experience attacks from any of the three entrances at any time. Your base will be quite compact, so any super weapon will certainly find many targets. The narrow valleys into the central parts can sometimes be choking points when the computer tries to send troops in both directions.
