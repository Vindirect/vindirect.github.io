Massive Attack

Download this map for Generals Zero Hour

            

About

This is the latest map to be included in release 5.
It is a 3 vs 3 tournament playfield! :)
We love it!

Layout

Diagnoal design, temperate forest climate and water. Large base areas and extra building space behind bases in two corners. Central diagonal plateaus connected trough double bridges, making line of defense from corner to corner. Road network for easy navigation and visibility of passable routes. Buildings, bunkers, chemical bunkers, and harmful radiation clouds. Four islands critical to gameplay.

Supplies

One large supply dock at each starting position.
Four oil derricks and one oil refinery forming a line behind the three starting positions in opposite corners.
The four islands each hold either a supergeneral nuke or a USA command center (superweapon or laser).
You will have to think twice about how to make use of these command centers. ;)
Within the central defense diagonal there are:
- 12 large supply docks
- 8 oil derricks
- 4 oil refineries
- 2 hospitals
- 2 reinforcement pads
- 2 USA Airforce airfields
- 2 GLA Toxin supply stashs
- 4 Crusader crates
- 6 $ crates

AI

Works very well!
Sometimes when capturing a building on an island near your enemy, that enemy will try to reach the island from the shore, and there is lag!
