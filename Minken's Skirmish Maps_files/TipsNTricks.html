Tips & Tricks

Change Time of Day!

- Don't like the light settings on a particular map? Launch Worldbuilder, open the map and hit ctrl+D a few times to change time of day. Save.

Custom Settings!

- If you want increased performance to speed up the game, switch to low detail or custom settings (I prefer having cloud ground shadows and smooth water).

Garrison in the building!

- To make the computer garrison in a specific building, select the building in Worldbuilder and name it "PlayerX GarrisonY" (without the ""). X is the number of the team that you want to garrison in the building and Y is 1, 2, or 3 (You can have at least three different buildings for each team to garrison in).

Create Waves!

- To create your own waves try WAKedit! To disable waves simply delete the .wak file in the map folder.

Check out this map!

- Wanna improve your maps? Download the map Flash Fire by the C&C Generals team! Check it out in Worldbuilder.
