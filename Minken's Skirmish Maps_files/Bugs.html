Bugs

There are lots of known minor map bugs that I am not going to list here.

1. Zero Hour needs larger base areas than Generals for additional buildings. Because of this, older (smaller) maps may not work very well with Zero Hour.

2. All maps may experience lag (slow-motion, freeze) due to any of the following situations:

A. The computer tries to capture buildings that are out of reach (rare).

B. Zero Hour units with special features (such as climbing mountain walls or jumping off cliffs) may get stuck.

C. Jarmen Kell shoots a vehicle in a choke point or on a bridge. Other units are blind to this vehicle.

D. Red guards (or mini-gunners) in a troop crawler vehicle may suffer total confusion when the vehicle is destroyed...

E. Computer-controlled troups gather behind cliffs or mountain ridges (rare).

3. The computer never learned to handle his particle cannon. ;)
