About

Temptetion Island face lift.

Layout

Based on the original map Temptation Island. The four canals now have double bridges. Each canal bridge has a gun tower on each side. More supplies and money crates added.

Supplies

Large supply docks and additional base supply piles. One oil derrick near each base and two oil derricks in the center. Four 1000$ crates around the oil refinery on the central island. One reinforcement pad near each base.

AI

USA and GLA perform well but China factions tend to have a slow start.
