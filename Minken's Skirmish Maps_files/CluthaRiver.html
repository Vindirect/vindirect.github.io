Clutha River

Download this map for Generals Zero Hour or Generals

        


About

This map was made in less than 5 hours - I think I'm getting the hang of it. Wanted to make a river, simple as that. Clutha River is a well-known fisherman's paradise near Wanaka on the New Zealand south island. I was thinking of a 6-player map, but this one is only 300x300 in size. The two lower base areas are a bit smaller than the top ones. It's not very symmetrical in terms of resources either. I made the terrain and texture work in a hurry so it's not excellent. A cool 2on2 map :)

Layout

The northwest corner of the map is a hill. Here is where the river starts. A river flows from the mountains and into a lake in the southeast corner. There are two bridges and a passage through the mountains over the river. Some small civilian camps and a few houses along the river. A boat patrolling along the river banks.

Supplies

One supply dock per base area. Oil derricks and oil refineries close to each starting position. Some smaller supply piles near the river and a supply dock in the northwest corner on the hill.

AI

Have not tested much but should work well.
