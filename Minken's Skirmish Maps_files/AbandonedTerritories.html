Abandoned Territories

Download this map for Generals Zero Hour or Generals

          

About

A medium-detailed 400x400 dirt/desert 6-player map.
Note: Worldbuilder doesn't work well with bridges :(

Layout

Some abandoned mines and outposts in a desert. A plateau in the north and a river flowing through the landscape. Mountain ridges and water separating the base areas. The villages are nearly destroyed. The country has been seriously injured by GLA scud storms and nukes. There are dangerous warheads in the field. A couple of chemical bunkers are ready to blow up. The locals have all fled or hung themselves behind the hospital on the hill. The coast in the south has been spared. Only a few farmers have stayed here in the river deltas.

Supplies

You start out with one supply dock. There are oil derricks and oil refineries in the deserts and towards the middle. A hospital has barely survived the chaos.
