About the Author

Hogmark resides in Sweden. Besides Generals, he has designed maps and levels for Counter-Strike: Source, Unreal Tournament, Strike-Force (UT mod), and some older games including Red Alert 2. Just for the fun of it! :)

The name Minken has been used for ages. Direct translation from Swedish would be: The Mink

The maps here are not designed to look overly natural or realistic but to offer fair play and lots of fun!
