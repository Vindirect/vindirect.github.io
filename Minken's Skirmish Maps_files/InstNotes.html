Installation Notes

Simply unzip the downloads to your Maps folder!

For Command & Conquer Generals Zero Hour, it should be something like:

My Documents\Command and Conquer Generals Zero Hour Data\Maps\

For Command & Conquer Generals, it should be something like:

My Documents\Command and Conquer Generals Data\Maps

Each map (with accompanying files) should reside in its own folder inside the Maps folder.

Good luck! :)
