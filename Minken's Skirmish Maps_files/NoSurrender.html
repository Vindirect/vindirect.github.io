No Surrender

Download this map for Generals Zero Hour

        

About

Six years have passed since I made my first skirmish map. I do not surrender! :)

Layout

A highly symmetrical (as always!) 400x400 4-player map with a treasured island in the center.

Supplies

You start out with one supply dock. Additional supply piles nearby. A couple of oil derricks near the water, and one oil derrick in each corner of the map. Supply docks for expansion reside in all corners of the map. The central island holds two supply docks, two oil refineries, one reinforcement pad and a GLA supply stash!

AI

As usual, the map has been tested with all available armies, making for a good match! Attack paths lead into each base from four different directions. Gameplay may slow down if units get stuck, although this rarely happens.
