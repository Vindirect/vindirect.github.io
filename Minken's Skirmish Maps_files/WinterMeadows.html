Winter Meadows

Download this map for Generals Zero Hour or Generals

        

About

This is just another version of Ireland Meadows. The green textures have been replaced with winter. Some of the trees have been replaced, and sounds have changed.

Layout

The players will have one opponent near when they start, and the two others on a safe (?) distance. There are a number of ways to advance on this map. First, there are four valleys that meet in the center of the map. Bridges cross each valley to connect the surrounding mountain sides. The upper left and lower right corner of the map holds higher ground with more resources.

Supplies

One supply dock and three supply piles close to each starting position. Additional supplies in the center and corners of the map, and in the mountains. The upper left and lower right corner holds oil refineries and a surplus of supplies. There is also an oil refinery in the middle. Four oil derricks are present.

AI

Each base has four entrances. AI may use any of them. Oil derricks will be captured by the computer, as will the oil refinery in the center. The oil refineries in the mountains might not be captured. Computers that fight successfully will move into the mountains to secure and harvest additional supplies.
