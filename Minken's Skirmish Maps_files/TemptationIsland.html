Temptation Island

Download this map for Generals Zero Hour or Generals

        

About

This is another small nifty map with a moderate amount of resources. The terrain was built using a high feather value. Made in just a few mouse strokes. The minimap looks like crap, but it's not such a bad map after all.

Layout

A flat landscape of grass and some willow trees. Water separates the outer perimeters of each base area. A central island. Bridges to connect the continents to eachother. There are a few chemical bunkers in this map. When destroyed these bunkers will spread a cloud of chemicals, harmful for all nearby units. Some buildings are leaking poison, so watch your ground troops carefully.

Supplies

One supply dock per base area. Oil derricks towards the center within close range. The central island has an oil refinery, but it can be difficult to capture (as you will notice :). There are some extra supplies in the center as well as some misplaced UN supplies. You can race to the middle to grab the candy, but I would recommend using a fast vehicle that burns slowly.

AI

Works well, especially when playing three computers. In most cases the computer will attack from the central island, a dangerous route - but watch all the bridges or base entrances. Oil derricks will be catured, but the central oil refinery stands untouched!
